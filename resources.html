Resources for Continued Learning

Continue your self-compassion journey with these resources for deeper exploration and practice.

Books

  • "Self-Compassion" by Kristin Neff

    The foundational book on self-compassion research and practice.

  • "The Mindful Self-Compassion Workbook"

    By Kristin Neff and Christopher Germer – Practical exercises.

Websites

  • self-compassion.org

    Kristin Neff's official site with free exercises and resources.

  • centerformsc.org

    Center for Mindful Self-Compassion – Training programs.

Apps & Audio

  • Insight Timer

    Free app with numerous self-compassion meditations.

  • Self-Compassion.org Audio

    Free guided meditations on Kristin Neff's website.

Programs & Training

  • Mindful Self-Compassion (MSC) Course

    8-week structured program available in-person and online.

  • Self-Compassion Workshops

    Shorter workshops and retreats offered worldwide.

Self-compassion is a journey, not a destination. Be patient with yourself as you explore these resources.
