Optional Writing Support

If you find the compassionate letter writing challenging, here are some additional supports to help you access your self-compassionate voice.

Sentence Starters

If you're having trouble getting started, try filling in these sentence starters:

Template

Dear ,

I notice you're struggling with .

It makes sense that you feel because .

You're not alone in this. Many people .

One thing I appreciate about you is .

A kind thing you might try is .

With compassion,

Gentle Reminder

This letter is just for you. There's no right or wrong way to write it. The most important thing is that it feels supportive and kind to you.

Alternative Approaches

If writing doesn't feel comfortable, try one of these alternatives:

  • Audio Recording

    Record yourself speaking compassionately, as if talking to a friend. Listen back to receive the compassion.

  • Visual Expression

    Draw or create a visual representation of compassion. Use colors, shapes, or symbols that feel supportive.

  • Compassionate Dialogue

    Imagine a conversation between your critical voice and your compassionate voice. What would each say?

  • Physical Gesture

    Place a hand on your heart and speak or think compassionate phrases while feeling the warmth of your hand.
