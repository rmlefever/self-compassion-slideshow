Group Reflection

Sharing our experiences with self-compassion can deepen our understanding and help us feel less alone in our practice. Consider these questions for group discussion.

Discussion Questions

  • What was it like to practice self-compassion today? What feelings or thoughts came up?

  • Which element of self-compassion (mindfulness, common humanity, or self-kindness) feels most natural to you? Which feels most challenging?

  • What resistance or barriers do you notice when trying to be compassionate toward yourself?

  • How might practicing self-compassion impact your daily life and relationships?

Facilitation Tips

  • Remind participants that sharing is optional; they can pass at any time.

  • Acknowledge that self-compassion can bring up difficult emotions for some people.

  • Validate all experiences, whether positive, negative, or neutral.

  • Highlight common themes that emerge in the discussion.

  • Gently redirect if someone begins to give advice rather than sharing their own experience.
