Common Self-Critical Thoughts

"I'm not good enough."

Often appears when facing challenges or comparing ourselves to others

"I always mess things up."

Tends to emerge after making mistakes, reinforcing a negative self-image

"I shouldn't feel this way."

Criticizes our emotional responses, creating secondary suffering

"What's wrong with me?"

Questions our fundamental worth or suggests we're broken

Group Reflection

Take a moment to notice your own self-critical thoughts. What patterns do you recognize?

• When do your self-critical thoughts tend to appear most strongly?

• What tone of voice do they use? Does it remind you of anyone?

• How do these thoughts make you feel emotionally and physically?
