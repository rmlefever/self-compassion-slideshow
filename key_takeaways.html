Key Takeaways

Self-Compassion Is a Skill

Like any skill, self-compassion can be learned and strengthened with practice. It's not about being perfect, but about showing up for yourself with kindness again and again.

Three Elements Work Together

Mindfulness helps us recognize our suffering, common humanity connects us to others, and self-kindness offers the warmth and support we need. All three components work together to create true self-compassion.

Small Practices Make a Difference

Even brief moments of self-compassion throughout your day can gradually transform your relationship with yourself. Start with small, manageable practices and build from there.

Self-Compassion Builds Resilience

Research shows that self-compassion helps us bounce back from difficulties, reduces anxiety and depression, and supports our overall wellbeing and recovery.
